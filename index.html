Restrooms
We looked for restrooms that are either:
  • At the location
  • Nearby (in a building)
  • Within a few minutes away
Single-Stall Restrooms
To locate single-stall restrooms, we used the list of Gender-Neutral Bathrooms on the UW campus.
Comfortable seating
Comfortable seating is personal, but we looked for places that have:
  • Benches or wide seats
  • With backrests
  • Usually made of a soft material like wood or plastic
Frequent seating
Frequent seating: you can quickly get to a seat from most areas, for whenever you want to sit.
Can lie down
We looked for:
  • Benches long enough to lie down on
  • Open grassy areas
Smooth surfaces
We looked for places without:
  • Extremely bumpy sidewalks
  • Gravel
  • Dirt
Views of nature
We looked for places with views of:
  • Trees
  • Plants
  • Other nature scenery
Quiet spaces
Private / enclosed spaces
Low traffic
Moveable tables and chairs
